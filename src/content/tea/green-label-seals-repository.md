The Green Label period lasted roughly from 1977 to 1982-1986 (the end date is arguable), spanning nearly a decade. 
Because Hong Kong businesses entered Yixing during this time, production increased dramatically, resulting in a large 
variety of base stamps.

In <a href="/tea/f1-seals/">"Yixing Factory 1 Seals"</a> post, I tried to illustrate the most common seals for the era. 
This post is a repository of all the seals' variations from the Green Label era which will be updated as soon as a new 
seal comes to my knowledge.

At the moment, 143 seals are known—and counting!

Full credit goes to the many dedicated Yixing enthusiasts who helped gather and document these seals in one place. Their 
collective effort made this repository possible.
